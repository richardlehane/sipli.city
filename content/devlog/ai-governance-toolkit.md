In collaboration with [Recordkeeping Innovation (RKI)](https://www.records.com.au), I developed an Artificial Intelligence Governance Toolkit. This comprises an AI Governance Maturity Model, risk rating tool, AI systems register, information managers' checklist and a model corporate AI policy. More information about RKI's AI governance services is available [here](https://records.com.au/artificial-intelligence-governance/).
