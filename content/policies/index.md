This Privacy Policy identifies the personal information collected about you and why, describes what is done with that personal information, how it is used and disclosed, and your rights to access and request corrections or deletion of information.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Siplicity application, this website (https://sipli.city), and customer interactions (e.g. email enquiries, support tickets). 

The Privacy Policy does not apply to third party services.

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information, which is defined as any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

All such information is subject to their privacy policy. This information is used by the payment service provider for processing orders, fraud prevention, and issuing refunds.

Personal information is not shared with third parties for use in their marketing. Personal information is not sold to third parties.

Marketing and product information relating to Siplicity (e.g. information about product updates) may be sent to you, but only with your prior consent.
